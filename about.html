About the band

Midnight Catch

For Midnight Catch it is the cheering of our fans which we want. The only thing which boosts our confidence is your participation in our fun. The one and only factor which gives our morale a sky-high jump is the spark in your eyes during the entertainment we provide you. Midnight Catch is all about the entertainment of our fans. We make sure your every guest is fully entertained. We practice to leave a never dying mark on your nerves. Our Irish band only knows touching the right cords of your heart.

Johnathan D. Sullivan has made music his whole life. Not only does John own the country's largest entertainment company. He is also responsible for countless serenading ballads and belting out the up tempo pop anthems throughout the many decades.

- Johnny Dee

David McKenna comes from a long line of musicians. Taking his craft very seriously, you can be assured of a full and professional performance that will make any night exceptional. Having Parents and grandparents that were musicians, I guess you could say it runs in the family.

- David

Bass - Started playing at 4 years old. Ricardo loves Bob Dylan, Rolling Stones, and all the classics. Midnight Catch hits are complemented by the jazzy tones of Ricardo O'Brien on keys and the smooth bass.

- Ricardo

Drums - Very passionate about his instument. Of course Mightnight Catch's hits would not be the same without by the steady beats of drummer Paul Doyle we have all come to love. Paul has dedicated his spare time to teaching his craft.

- Paul
